3 easy&tasty ideas for Snack meal

Baked Spicy Potatoes
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 6 Units
Olive Oil 3 Spoon
Red Chili Paprika 1 spoon
Salt 1 spoon

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees. First peel the potatoes and then cut them into strips. Put the olive oil in a small bowl and mix it with the spices. Take a baking pan and spread baking paper. Slice all the potato slices and with a brush apply olive oil with the spices. Put them in the oven for half an hour. Enjoy!

Nut Cake
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2 units
Sugar 1/2 cup
Oil 1/3 cup
Orange juice 1/2 cup
Flour 1 cup
Baking powder 1 teaspoon
Cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon
Ground Walnuts 3/4 cup

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees and grease an English cake pan. Mix the eggs with the sugar. Add oil and orange juice and mix. Add self-raising flour, cinnamon powder, nuts and coconut and mix lightly until the ingredients are combined. Pour the mixture into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.

Chocolate Balls
Ingredients Quantity
Biscuits 300g
Dark chocolate 2pc
Butter 50gr
Cocoa powder 3 tablespoons
Sugar 3 spoons
Milk 1/2 cup
Coconut 1pc

Place the butter and chocolate cubes in a glass bowl and melt in the microwave for a few seconds. Remove and mix until a smooth chocolate mixture is obtained. Mix half a cup of milk with the cocoa powder and sugar. After the sugar has melted, pour into the chocolate bowl. Add the biscuit crumbs to the bowl and mix well Using your hands, form balls and dip in a small plate with coconut. Wrap in plastic wrap and store in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours before serving.
