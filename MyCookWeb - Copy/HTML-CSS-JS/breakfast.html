4 easy&tasty ideas for breakfast meal

Mushroom omelette with an indulgent toast
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4 units
Mushroom 3 units
Feta cheese 100gr
Tomato 1 unit
Onion 1 unit
Salt 1 spoon
Cumin 1 spoon
Pune 1 spoon
Black paper 1 spoon

First you add oil to the pan and cut the mushrooms and onions. Then you add them to the pan and fry them for about 7 minutes. In another bowl you add the eggs and mix them with the spices. After that you add the batter. After about 10 minutes the omelette is ready and we will start working on the toast. First put it in the toaster for about 5 minutes. After that, spread the chosen spread, add the feta cheese and the cut tomato. Sprinkle some salt. And here she has a delicious and nutritious breakfast!

Egg in hole
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2 units
Mushroom 3 units
Feta cheese 100gr
Tomato 1 unit
Onion 1 unit
Salt 1 spoon
Cumin 1 spoon
Black paper 1 spoon

First you take a slice of bread and take out all the soft part inside it. Then you take oil and add it to the pan. Then you start frying the bread. At the same time you pour 2 eggs into a plate and add your favorite spices. After you flip the bread, you also melt the batter. After about 7 minutes your meal is ready. With appetite!

Shakshuka
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4 units
Mushroom 3 units
Feta cheese 100gr
Tomato 1 unit
Onion 1 unit
Salt 1 spoon
Cumin 1 spoon
Black paper 1 spoon

First you cut garlic, onion and tomatoes and start frying them in a pan. After about 7 minutes you add tomato paste, a cup of hot water and spices. After boiling the mixture in the pan, you lower the heat and add the eggs. Adding the eggs involves making a hole and adding the egg. Leave for slow frying for about 10 minutes and ready!
